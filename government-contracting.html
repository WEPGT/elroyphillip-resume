Core capabilities

WEP Global Tech provides hands-on, operations-focused technology support with an emphasis on uptime, observability, and secure access.

Cloud & Infrastructure

  • AWS cloud infrastructure deployment & hardening (VPC, IAM, VPN, security groups)
  • Infrastructure as Code (Terraform) for repeatable, auditable environments
  • Monitoring, alerting, and logging for cloud and hybrid environments
  • Secure remote access (VPN, WireGuard, bastion hosts) for admins and field teams

Systems & Networking

  • Windows and Linux systems administration for servers and endpoints
  • LAN / WAN design, firewall configuration, and segmenting sensitive networks
  • Small enterprise network design (MikroTik, managed switches, enterprise Wi-Fi)
  • Performance troubleshooting and optimization for on-prem and cloud workloads

Security & Compliance Support

  • Secure configuration baselines and access control (least privilege)
  • Logging and audit trails to support compliance and investigations
  • Backup, recovery, and continuity planning for critical systems
  • Vulnerability remediation in partnership with security teams

Broadcast / A/V & Special Environments

  • Support for control-room style, 24×7 environments
  • Integration of A/V, networking, and compute for production workflows
  • Coordination between engineering, operations, and vendor teams
  • Documentation and runbook creation for complex systems

Past performance & relevant experience

WEP Global Tech brings a blend of enterprise operations, systems administration, and hands-on engineering experience that translates directly into reliable support for government customers.

  • Enterprise operations & support: Experience supporting mission-critical production environments where minutes of downtime matter.
  • Cloud & automation: Design and deployment of AWS-based dashboards, logging pipelines, and automation to improve visibility into applications and infrastructure.
